After A&P, I found myself to have the great fortune to join the design team for Shoppers Supermarket (Super Valu) in Bowie, MD. I worked for Ivie & Associates officially. (based out of Flower Mound, TX which is by Dallas.)

They assigned me to this satellite location by Washington, D.C. Here also, I designed circulars and created more publications which added value daily. I even designed pages for Shop N Save and Farmfresh as well as spearheaded the El Tiempo Latino Full Page Newspaper Ad project during this time. (for Shoppers, through the Washington Post Newspaper.)

I was only there for two years because they packed up this satellite location to join again the Shoppers Corporate Office and “moved shop” to MN. They already had a full staff in MN so I moved back north to join forces with Clark Printing in Fairfield, NJ as my next adventure awaited...
